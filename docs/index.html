Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This privacy policy describes how we handle information in connection with our SMS-based personal assistant service (the "Service"). The Service allows you to send text messages to a personal phone number to receive automated responses and manage tasks, reminders, and other personal data.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with the Service via SMS, we may receive and store:

  • Your phone number (as provided by your carrier)
  • The content of messages you send to the Service
  • Timestamps of messages sent and received
  • Data you explicitly provide (e.g., tasks, reminders, notes)

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Process and respond to your SMS commands
  • Store tasks, reminders, notes, and other data you request
  • Deliver scheduled reminders and notifications back to you

Your data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Message content and associated data are stored in a private database accessible only to the operator of this Service. Data is retained for as long as the Service is in operation or until you request deletion.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses Twilio to send and receive SMS messages. Twilio may process your phone number and message content in accordance with their own privacy policy. No other third-party services receive your personal data.

5. Your Rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to the data stored about you
  • Deletion of your data from the Service
  • To opt out by replying STOP to any message

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including server-side authentication and encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) for all communications.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

This is a personal-use service. For questions or data requests, reply directly to the SMS number you use to contact the Service.

This policy applies to personal, non-commercial use of this SMS service.
